Durango, Mexico

Wetland ecosytems are indispensable for living organisms. All of us need water and wetlands contribute greatly to have healthy water afordable. And that is just one example, their existens benefit life in so many ways.

Durango state is at north-west-center (sort of) Mexico, it is one of the biggest states of the country and cover several types of ecosystems.

Dry condition prevalence in a big part of its territory, but still, we can find wetlands throughout the state.

And that is another amazing thing about wetlands, they can be found in almost everywere and comes in so many presentations.
